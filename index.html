About Me

Who I Am

I am a Product Designer and Photographer from Varanasi, currently based at MIT-WPU Pune. My journey began with a natural drive toward creativity, which led me to the world of design. Since then, I've focused on blending technical foundations with a strong visual narrative.

My perspective shifted significantly after joining the university's premier photography club. Managing large scale events and leading visual documentation didn't just sharpen my eye for composition it broadened my understanding of marketing, event management, and effective communication. This experience taught me how to view design through a wider lens, ensuring that every project I touch is both functional and impactful.

I've dedicated myself to studio projects and freelance work, allowing me to apply design thinking to real-world challenges. I am constantly evolving my toolkit through specialized courses, mastering new software, and solidifying my design basics.
